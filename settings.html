BlueLight Settings

Dim the screen

Lowering your screen's brightness can significantly reduce blue light exposure.

Use Night Shift/ Blue light filter

Many devices have built-in features that shift the screen's color temperature to warmer tones in the evening.

Wear Blue light Blocking Glasses

These glasses have special lenses that filter out blue light, reducing eye strain and improving sleep quality.

Intensity

Adjust the strength of the filter to control how much blue light is filtered out.

Color Temperature

Change the color temperature of the screen to a warmer tone, reducing the amount of blue light.

Schedule

Set a schedule for the filter to turn on and off automatically, such as during the evening hours.
